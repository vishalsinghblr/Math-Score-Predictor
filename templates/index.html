Student Exam Performance Predictor

Welcome!

This application helps you predict a student's math score based on various factors, including their writing and reading scores, gender, ethnicity, parental education level, and more. It's designed to support educators and students in identifying key areas for improvement.

Key Features

  • Accurate predictions based on advanced machine learning algorithms.
  • Simple and intuitive interface for easy data entry.
  • Supports diverse student demographics and data points.
  • Actionable insights for educators and parents to improve performance.
